Springrolls Syndicate

Springrolls Syndicate is a work in progress faction mod for the game Starsector made by me and Julius Kristanto. It is made using Java and Starsector's object interaction system.

New faction and star system

The mod adds a new cuisine themed faction and star system, with a whole new trading focus being planned to enrich the Starsector's economy system.

Unique Planets

With the background of the faction having multiple catastrophies, this leads to the faction having a unique capital planet look. Differing from the usual Starsector's vanilla planets.

New Ships

This mod also adds several new ships to the faction. The new ships are inspired by asian automotives, and are supposed to conform with the faction's playstyle.
